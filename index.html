About This Bridge

The Classical↔Quantum Bridge maps threats, security controls, and architectural components between the ONI (Open Neurosecurity Interoperability) classical 14-layer model and the QIF (Quantum Indeterministic Framework) quantum 7-band hourglass.

This visualization demonstrates how two complementary security frameworks work together to provide comprehensive BCI protection — classical for established threats and quantum for next-generation security.

Qinnovate (Standards) develops the open frameworks
Mindloft (Products) implements them commercially

Think: W3C (Qinnovate) vs Chrome (Mindloft)
